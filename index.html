Unordered list "ul"

Unordered list (often called a "bullet list" - but remember, there's no formatting in HTML!) Used for lists where the items have no critical order of execution Always contains "li" tags!

  • Something
  • Another thing
  • Also this one
MDN - Unordered List
********************************************************************************************************************

List item "li"

Always nested inside a "ul" or "ol" tag!

  • Something
  • Another thing
  • Also this one
MDN - List Item
********************************************************************************************************************

Ordered list "ol"

Used for lists where the order is important (like a list of instructions). Often called a "number list" - but remember there's no formatting in HTML! Always contains "li" tags!

  1. Something
  2. Another thing
  3. Also this one
